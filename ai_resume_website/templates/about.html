About TalentLens

AI-powered career intelligence platform

What We Do

TalentLens analyzes resumes using Artificial Intelligence to extract skills, evaluate resume strength, identify skill gaps, and match users with the most suitable career paths.

Why It Matters

Many students are unsure about their career direction. TalentLens bridges the gap between education and industry by providing data-driven career insights.

Future Vision

We aim to build a connected career ecosystem where users can improve their skills, connect with similar profiles, and grow professionally.
